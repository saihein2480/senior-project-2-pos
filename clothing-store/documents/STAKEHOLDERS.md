Stakeholders

Owner has full administrative control over the system including managing products, inventory, customers, carts, sales transactions, expenses, shop branches, staff accounts, manage barcode and system settings.

Customer can browse available products, check shop locations, and review the terms and conditions.

Staff (Cashier) has restricted permissions and is allowed to view products, process sales transactions and refunds, handle payments, manage customer information, and access limited system settings.

Manager has comprehensive administrative access to the system, including product and inventory management, customer management, sales and expense tracking, branch management, barcode management, and system configuration. However, the Manager does not have permission to create, modify, or delete staff accounts.
